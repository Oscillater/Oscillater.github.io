基于hexo的自建博客的latex显示问题

个人博客使用“致远”主题，并使用第三方插件“hexo-filter-mathjax”进行服务端渲染。
目前发现上传含latex文档时遇到以下问题

  • 在使用latex显示分数时，例如\frac{{1}}{2}这样的式子会在generate hexo 文件时就出现问题，需要把它们改成不带括号的样子\frac{1}{2}。
  • 根据其github主页上的提示，发现由于latex与markdown语法的冲突，带有下划线的式子需要进行重新处理：例如\mu_{0}需要改为\mu\_0
    针对这个问题利用gpt+python+正则表达式写了个脚本：
import re

with open('test.md', 'r',encoding='utf-8') as file:
    content = file.read()

latex_statements_inline = re.findall(r'\$.*?\$', content)

processed_statements = []
for statement in latex_statements_inline:
    statement = statement.replace('_', '\_')
    #替换'_'为'\_'，解决latex语法与markdown冲突的问题
    statement = re.sub(r'{{([^}]*)}}',r'{\1}',statement)
    #删除公式内无用的括号，方便latex显示插件解析
    processed_statements.append(statement)

for original, processed in zip(latex_statements_inline, processed_statements):
    content = content.replace(original, processed)

latex_statements_outline = re.findall(r'\$\$.*?\$\$', content, re.DOTALL)
# 对行间latex语句做同样的操作
processed_statements = []
for statement in latex_statements_outline:
    statement = statement.replace('_', '\_')
    statement = re.sub(r'{{([^}]*)}}', r'{\1}', statement)
    processed_statements.append(statement)

for original, processed in zip(latex_statements_outline, processed_statements):
    content = content.replace(original, processed)


with open('test.md', 'w',encoding='utf-8') as file:
    file.write(content)
